Applause

What

Applause is a like button that's really simple to set up.

How

  1. Add <div id="applause"></div> where you want the button.
  2. Add <script src="http://applause.jacksondc.com/script.js"></script> after your body.

Demo

Details

Applause automatically prevents double-voting. It's loosely inspired by Kudos and Open Kudos. Email me if you have questions.
